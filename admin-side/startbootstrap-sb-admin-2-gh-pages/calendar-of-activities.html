2 Parada ng Lechon Festival -Balayan town celebrates the feast of St. John the Baptist every June 24th with a parade of roast pig (lechon). In parody of the time-honored ritual of baptism, townsfolk throw water on each other during the parade in the spirit of fun. After the lechon has been blessed, everyone sits down to partake in the feast. June 24 Batangas Balayan 08/16/23 08/16/23
3 Tapusan Festival -Starting on the first of May, floral offerings are laid before an old cross in the town of Alitagtag. The honoring of the Holy Cross peaks on the last day of May with a procession during which the cross is mounted on a beautifully decorated float and drawn through the streets with other flower-bedecked floats carrying Marian images. May 31 Batangas Alitagtag 08/16/23 08/16/23
